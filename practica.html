Articles (a, an, the)

A1

A/An = no específico. The = específico. An antes de sonido de vocal. Sin artículo para cosas en general.

ArtículoUsoEjemplo
aAntes de consonantea book, a car
anAntes de vocalan apple, an hour
theAlgo específicothe sun, the book on the table
--General / abstractoI like music

1. She is ___ doctor.

2. I want ___ apple.

3. ___ sun is very bright today.

4. He is ___ honest person.

5. I like ___ music.

Prepositions of Place

A1

Las preposiciones de lugar indican dónde está algo: in (dentro), on (sobre), under (debajo), next to (al lado), between (entre), behind (detrás), in front of (enfrente).

1. The cat is ___ the box. (dentro)

2. The book is ___ the table. (sobre)

3. The shoes are ___ the bed. (debajo)

4. The pharmacy is ___ the bank and the school.

5. The park is ___ the hospital. (enfrente)

Resultado

0/5

Giving Directions

A1

Aprende a pedir y dar direcciones en inglés. Vocabulario clave: turn left/right, go straight, go past, it's on the corner.

Turn left Gira a la izquierda
Turn right Gira a la derecha
Go straight Sigue derecho
Go past Pasa por / Pasa de largo
On the corner En la esquina
Across from Enfrente de

1. Excuse me, how do I ___ to the train station?

2. ___ straight ahead and then turn left.

3. The supermarket is ___ the corner of Main Street.

4. Go ___ the church and the bank is on your right.

5. The café is ___ the bookstore. (al otro lado de la calle)
